About this site

The {{ conf.tm }} calendar is work in progress and r&d, trying to find a solution that works for everyone. Currently the toolkit and service being used is Google Calendar as this has all the widgets, feeds etc etc.

The dissadvantage of this is that a google account is aqquired. This does not mean that you need a new email address etc, but instead use your existing address to create a "token" with google.

The advantage is that it makes access to google services including access easy and for the developers its much easier to code and more secure.
