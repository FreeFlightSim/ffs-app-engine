About this site

This FlightGear Calendar is located at
http://www.google.com/calendar/hosted/freeflightsim.org/render?cid=fg@freeflightsim.org

fg@freeflightsim.org is an user account with a public calendar on the domain. This site works by remotely adding users in edit mode to the calendar, enabling maintainance of entries.

Its R & D and work in progress, trying to find a solution that works for everyone, is easily managable and updateable. Currently the toolkit and service being used is Google Calendar as this has all the widgets, authentication, feeds etc.

The disadvantage is that a google account is required. This does not mean that you need a new email address etc, but instead can use your existing address to create a "token" with google.

The advantage is that it makes access to google services including access easy. For the developers its much easier to code and more secure.
