About this site

The FlightGear Calendar is located at
http://www.google.com/calendar/hosted/freeflightsim.org/render?cid=fg@freeflightsim.org

This calendar is R & D and work in progress, trying to find a solution that works for everyone, and is easily updateable. Currently the toolkit and service being used is Google Calendar as this has all the widgets, feeds etc etc.

The disadvantage is that a google account is required. This does not mean that you need a new email address etc, but instead use your existing address to create a "token" with google.

The advantage is that it makes access to google services including access easy; for the developers its much easier to code and more secure.
