FlightGear Aircraft

Once of the huge benefits of this flight simulator is the wide variety of aircraft available for you to try out.

Currently you can fly the 1903 Wright Flyer, strange flapping wing "ornithopters", a 747 and A320, various military jets, and several light singles.

As a lifetime member you get full access to ALL the planes, helicopters and gliders for free. No costly upgrades, no paying for each plane. This is just another great reason to get FlightGear now.
