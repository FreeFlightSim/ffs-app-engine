About this site

Inspired by this forum posting - Website for requesting a controller

You usually only need air traffic controllers when you are taking off or landing (even though there are center controllers), so I thought of making a website which allows you to request controllers when you are going to takeoff or land. And with flightgear's terrasync and the ability for controllers to teleport anywhere such a service could be possible. This could also be helpful for people who are ATC, because they dont have to stay at one place for a long time just to serve 1 or 2 pilots.

I love designing websites, so I have already started on the layout of the site, which you can check out here: http://nafis.us.to/fg_atc. I am currently working on the actual script which would allow the guys logged in as ATC to view requests from pilots. Its not very hard so I could finish the whole site in about a week, except for the next two weeks I will be very busy (so busy I might not even be able to go on flightgear!).

Anyway, I hope that this could help flightgear get closer to becoming similar to vatsim, and that im not just being a noob. :|
